# Vocabulary Practice

**Vocabulary Practice** is a simple, kid-friendly mobile app that helps children learn and review vocabulary words through reading, listening, quizzes, and spelling activities.

The app is built for **Android and iOS**. It works **offline** after installation and keeps everything on your device — no accounts, no ads, and no internet required to practice.

---

## Who is it for?

- **Children** who are building vocabulary and may still be developing reading skills
- **Parents and teachers** who want a straightforward practice tool without logins, subscriptions, or data collection

Each learner on a shared device can have their own **local profile** (for example, "Alex" or "Mia") so siblings or classmates can track progress separately.

---

## What can you do in the app?

### 1. Pick a learner profile
Create or choose a profile when you open the app. Profiles are stored only on the device — they are not online accounts.

### 2. Choose a word set
Browse vocabulary sets, search by name, and filter by grade level. Each set groups related words with definitions and examples.

### 3. Practice in four ways

| Mode | What it does |
|------|----------------|
| **Learn Words** | Review words and definitions with optional read-aloud support |
| **Quiz** | Match a definition to the correct word (multiple choice) |
| **Spell It** | Spell the word that matches a given definition using letter tiles |
| **Type It** | Type the word from memory for an extra recall challenge |

Read-aloud uses your device’s built-in text-to-speech so children can hear words and definitions even before they can read them comfortably.

### 4. Track progress
The app remembers each profile’s progress per word set — which words have been reviewed, quizzed, and spelled. Progress stays on the device and survives app restarts.

---

## How the app is designed

- **Local only** — profiles, settings, and progress are saved on your phone or tablet
- **No backend** — there is no server, API, or cloud database
- **No personal data collection** — we do not receive names, progress, or usage data from the app
- **No login** — no email, password, or signup
- **No ads** — practice without interruptions
- **Works offline** — vocabulary and practice modes do not need an internet connection

Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored profiles and progress. There is no cloud backup.

---

## Typical flow

```
Open app → Choose profile → Pick a word set → Practice (Learn / Quiz / Spell It / Type It) → See progress
```

From a word set, you can also view the full word list and reset progress for that set or profile if you want to start over.

---

## Settings

Parents and teachers can adjust options such as:

- Text-to-speech speed
- Auto-read for words and quiz prompts
- Whether **Type It** is required to mark a word complete
